# Code of Conduct

Zynksec adopts the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** as its code of conduct. The full
text lives at:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Summary

We are committed to making participation in Zynksec a welcoming and respectful
experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience
level.

### What we ask of participants

- Show empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Take responsibility and apologize to those affected by our mistakes.
- Focus on what is best for the community as a whole.

### What is not acceptable

Any behavior that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
professional setting is not acceptable in Zynksec spaces. This includes
personal attacks, insults, derogatory comments, and publishing others' private
information without permission. The full list of examples is in the
Contributor Covenant linked above.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all Zynksec spaces — the repository, GitHub
Discussions, Issues, Pull Requests, and any official communication channels —
and to anyone representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Concerns about conduct can be reported privately to:

**conduct@zynksec.dev**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The
reporter's privacy will be respected.

Enforcement follows the Contributor Covenant Community Impact Guidelines
(correction → warning → temporary ban → permanent ban) depending on severity
and pattern.
